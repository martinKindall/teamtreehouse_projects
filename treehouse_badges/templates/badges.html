Help & About

This app is intended for the teamtreehouse members, in order to follow badges earnings through the year. Our profiles info is public (thanks teamtreehouse!), that's why this app is non-intrusive and doesn't require your password. If you are a member and don't know your username, you can go to your profile in https://teamtreehouse.com.

Once you go to your profile, check the name that appears after the domain: https://teamtreehouse.com/username. Teamtreehouse gives us access to our profile in JSON format info if we write the request like this: https://teamtreehouse.com/username.json.
